Fashion Nova is a Los Angeles-based clothing retailer that specializes in inexpensive streetwear, especially modern, stylish women's apparel for all body types. They cater to Instagram and are known for their savvy on the platform. Spend enough time on Instagram, and Fashion Nova will inevitably pop up. This fast fashion retailer was founded in 2006 and exploded in popularity in the late 2010s. Fashion Nova is noted for their Instagram marketing (their handle is @fashionnova and they boast over 16 million followers). They have a reputation for selling clothes worn by the “Instagram baddie”—impossibly cool, curvy women on Instagram.
